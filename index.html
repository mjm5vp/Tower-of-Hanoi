Tower of Hanoi

The objective of the puzzle is to move the entire pile of stones to another platform, obeying the following three rules:

  1. Only one stone can be moved at a time.
  2. A stone can only be moved if it is the uppermost stone on a pile.
  3. No stone may be placed on top of a smaller stone.
